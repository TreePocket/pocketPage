# The LIon King

# Dad? I'm not supposed to go up here.

be supposed to do sth 应该做某事

up here 在一个特定的地方

Kingdom 王国

the light touches 阳光触及的地方

Look, 喂 听我说 常为不悦时唤起他人注意

# You rule all of that?

# Yes.But a king's time as ruler ... rises and falls like the sun.

a king's time as ruler 一个国王的统治

rises 升起 falls 落下

# One day,Simba... the sun will set on my time here... and will rise with you as the new king.

One day 某天

set 落下 sun set 日落

# All of this will belong to me?

belong to 属于

# It belongs to no one,but will be yours to protect.A great responsibility.

watering hole = water hole 水坑

# And that mountain? And beyond those shadows?

# You must not go there,Simba.

# But I thought a king could do whatever he wants. Take any territory.

take any territory 占领任何地方 territory 领地

# While others search for what they can take,a true king searches for what he can give

while 对比两件事物 ...而，...然而

search for 寻找

# Everything you see exists together in a delicate balance

Exist together 共存

delicate 熟练的 需要技巧的 微妙的

# as king,you need to understand that balance,and respect all the creatures from the crawling ant to the leaping antelope

Craw 爬行 leap 跳跃 antelope美洲羚羊

# Uh,we're the guys who saved your life

save one's life 拯救了某人的生命

# Risked everything. Fought off angry vultures! Hordes of them.Flocks.

risked everything 冒着丢掉一切的风险

fight off 击退 摆脱 抵抗

angry vultures 愤怒的秃鹫

hordes of 一大群

flock 羊或鸟群

# I'm Pumbaa. This is Timon. Did i mention that we saved your life?

# It doesn't matter.

# Whoa.How bleak is that? It doesn't even matter?

Bleak 不乐观的；无望的；暗淡的

# Like I don't have enough that I'm dealing with, right? Now his problems are my problems?

难道我操的心还不够多吗？他要死要活也要我管吗？

like = as if 好像，仿佛，似乎

# But we can help him.We're in a position to help him.

be in a position to do 有能力做某事，通常是因为你具有必要的经验、权威或金钱

As few words as possible 说的词越少越好

Wanna = want to

Talk about it 谈论

# Look,kid,we've all made mistakes.Uh,there must be something we can do, right?

we have all made mistakes 我们都犯过错误

there must be something we can do 我们一定可以做点什么

除非你们能改变过去

# That's our specialty.Kind of our thing.

specialty 特长，特产 specialty stores 特产商店

our thing 我们擅长的事情

# Well,to change the future,you gotta put your past behind you.Way behind.

想要改变未来，你必须把过去抛到脑后，抛得远远的

way 大大地，远远地

# Wrong! When the world turns its back on you,you turn your back on the world!
